# How to capture outsized returns

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** 6751a61e2cef43e09437968187a4d3a5

## Key Takeaways

- If you found a coin on Twitter, you have already missed most of the upside — coins shilled by big accounts have already had a large portion of their upside priced in.
- Outsized returns come from buying something no one else is talking about or has a thesis for, then profiting as more people come to believe what you already believe.
- Your best investments will be the hardest to buy in the moment — if it felt comfortable to buy, the upside is mostly already priced in.
- To be smart, early, or cheat (insider) are the three paths to outsized returns; being early is the most reliable — even idiots who bought SHIB early made fortunes.
- 10% of your decisions bring 90% of your profits — the right action in investing is almost always to do nothing except for the rare moments of free money on the floor.
- Outperformance is not a coin, it's a moment in time — any coin with the right setup (early, painful to buy, no Twitter consensus) can give outsized returns.
