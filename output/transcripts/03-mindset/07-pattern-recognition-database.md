# Pattern Recognition Database

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** 7667dd51b81549f58118b8a2c21c596c

## Key Takeaways

- A pattern recognition database is accumulated lived experience that allows your subconscious (intuition) to make fast, accurate investment decisions — good investors can determine if something is a good investment within 30 seconds.
- The hierarchy of decision-making is: copying Twitter < critical thinking < instinct < intuition. Intuition is the highest form because it is "perpetually accumulated wisdom from lived experiences which manifest in our subconscious mind."
- Instinct and investing are opposites — your instinct to follow the herd will make you lose money; counter-trading your instinct is actually useful.
- 80% of what you need to learn to make money can only be learned through firsthand experience — the goal is to make as many mistakes as possible with the least capital at risk.
- You have two choices to access good decision-making: follow the pro (work closely alongside someone who has the intuition) or become the pro (invest thousands of hours building your own database).
- The path to becoming the pro requires making so many mistakes that you eventually know what not to do, and the win rate tilts in your favor.
