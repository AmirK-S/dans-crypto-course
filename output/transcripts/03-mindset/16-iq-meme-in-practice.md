# IQ Meme In Practice

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** b988d8e14b224e1d8efab46e6ecfda74

## Key Takeaways

- The IQ meme shows that dumb people (buy because they like it) and smart people (buy because dumb people will like it) do the same thing in crypto — only the mid-IQ person misses out by thinking the investment is stupid.
- To buy the right coins, understand what your exit liquidity (the dumbest people at the end of the bull run) will buy — as the IQ of average market participants drops toward the top, meme coins dominate.
- The biggest problem for mid-IQ types is they fail to recognize that what they believe is correct is irrelevant — what matters is what the market will believe.
- You need to become a professional at predicting future narratives — understanding what others will think before they think it is how you profit.
- The alpha in crypto is emotional intelligence, not raw IQ — patience and discipline (waiting for price to come to you) matter more than analytical ability.
- Pick a side: either embrace your monkey (low IQ execution) or lean into high IQ (understand what the monkey will do) — the mid-curve position is the worst place to be.
