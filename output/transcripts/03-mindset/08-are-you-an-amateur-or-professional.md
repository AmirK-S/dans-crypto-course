# Are you an Amateur or Professional?

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** 4cd132e3a33748e8bd8db9127ca21eb5

## Key Takeaways

- If you approach crypto as an amateur you will lose money — the only outcome that leads to keeping money is approaching it as a professional.
- Amateurs have no system, no defined edge, and no back-tested strategy; professionals have all three and focus their attention on one segment of crypto where they consistently make money.
- Amateurs make emotional decisions, cut winners, and add to losers; professionals only buy when upside is asymmetric and add to winners while cutting losers.
- A professional optimizes for total portfolio return, not for individual coin multiples — a large position at 2x beats a tiny position at 100x.
- Everyone needs a mentor: every rich person the instructor knows either has or had a mentor, and being in high-level communities with alpha-sharing is essential.
- The two paths in crypto are: become the pro (takes thousands of hours) or follow the pro (use someone else's system).
