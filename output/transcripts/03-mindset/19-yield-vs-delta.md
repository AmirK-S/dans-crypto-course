# Yield vs Delta

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** 6b8280adcf2b4e03b13094168615b94e

## Key Takeaways

- There are only two ways to make money in crypto: yield (staking, mining, farming) and delta (price change from buying low and selling high); the instructor only does delta.
- Yield farming has three major risks that can cost you 100% of your capital: smart contract hacks, platform collapse, and liquidity pool drainage — collecting 5-10% yield while risking 100% of principal is a terrible risk/reward ratio.
- The biggest problem with yield in crypto is that your money gets locked, so you cannot sell at the optimal time — you can miss the entire exit and watch a 20x become a 90% loss.
- The Joe Blow example: $50k of Cardano at 10c grew to $1M earning 15% yield, then Cardano dropped 88% — it would take 48 years of yield to recover the capital loss.
- The best yield is price going up (delta) — buying at $1 and selling at $2 is a 100% yield with no lockup risk; delta always outperforms any yield scheme for active investors.
- Either do yield plus delta (earn yield on long-term holds) or just delta — never come to crypto purely for yield given the full capital risk involved.
