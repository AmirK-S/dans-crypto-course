# It’s not about the coin, it’s about the process

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** 6e48c54852a946bea70dbe0ed907e801

## Key Takeaways

- It is not about the coin — a great investor can make money on any coin because the process (entry, exit, sizing, emotional management) is what determines whether you profit.
- Even the best-performing coins from any bull run have caused people to lose money — it's never the coin itself, it's whether you have a process around the coin.
- Asking "what coin should I buy?" is the wrong question; "what process should I follow?" is the right one.
- Without a process you have no defined edge, which means crypto is a slot machine — every dollar in is statistically less than a dollar out.
- A great process gets you a good entry, a good exit, avoids making emotional decisions, and prevents buying consensus bets at the top.
- The most common outcome for those without a process: they buy the best coins but never sell, hold through the bear market, and give back all gains.
