# Emotions Destroy Profits

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** e91120a90a27495889435f5e92bbcb0e

## Key Takeaways

- Emotions destroy profits because humans are evolutionarily programmed to follow the herd — in investing this means buying at the top and selling at the bottom.
- You make money by buying when confidence is low (people are afraid) and selling when confidence is high — the opposite of instinct.
- Having a system that gives you signals holds you accountable and removes emotional decision-making, acting like "someone from the future telling you when to buy."
- A mentor who has been through market cycles before can bring you back to rational thinking when emotions take over.
- Reading old investing books teaches you what emotions you will feel at certain market stages before you experience them.
- Account for all scenarios before buying — if you already considered the possibility of a 30% drop, it won't emotionally affect you when it happens.
- Do not use leverage — it amplifies emotions to the point where most people can't handle the volatility and make irrational decisions.
