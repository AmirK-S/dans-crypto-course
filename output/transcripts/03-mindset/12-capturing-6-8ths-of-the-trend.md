# Capturing 6/8ths of the trend

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** 1b0091198bcf42f3b4133a9acd02dbf3

## Key Takeaways

- The goal is not to buy the exact bottom or sell the exact top — capturing six-eighths of the trend is both more achievable and captures 80% of the profits.
- Perfectionism in market timing costs you a good entry while chasing a perfect one, and ultimately removes most of the profit you could have made.
- Selling the top is harder than buying the bottom because altcoins can drop 50% in a week at the top, making even being one week late very costly.
- Buy when the downside is limited and the upside is asymmetric — this avoids the psychological trap of trying to time the exact bottom.
- Bottoms never feel like bottoms; the instructor always felt "we might go lower, let's wait" at exactly the bottom — the correct approach is to buy when downside is limited, not when it feels safe.
- Assume your portfolio is always worth 30% less than its current value, since you will never sell the exact top — this mindset prevents emotional over-attachment to peak portfolio value.
