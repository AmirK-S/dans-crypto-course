# The importance of volatility

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** 5fc9d9c91ea34996b60a1cd6ab2c589b

## Key Takeaways

- Volatility is not risk — it is just the standard deviation of prices along the way to an asset's ultimate value; permanent loss is the real risk.
- Between you and a 10-100x return are multiple 40-80% retracements; you must embrace this as a normal part of investing.
- Selling to avoid short-term downside volatility maximizes preservation of 20-40% of money while potentially missing 1000%+ upside — a bad trade-off.
- The biggest crypto gains happen in roughly 10-15 days per year; if you sell to avoid volatility you likely miss those days and underperform.
- Position yourself early into an asset's uptrend so temporary downside eats into profits, not initial capital — this makes volatility emotionally manageable.
- Your goal is to own assets with high volatility and high liquidity, as this combination is a recipe for outsized returns in crypto.
