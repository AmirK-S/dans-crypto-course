# Everyone pays their tuition

**Section:** Mindset
**Loom ID:** b36975f9467245a5b1d613616ff52d4e

## Key Takeaways

- Everyone who comes to crypto must pay a tuition fee — a minimum amount of losses, pain, and mistakes that cannot be avoided before you can consistently keep what you make.
- Investing in crypto is like a three-to-four year university degree: you cannot skip any semester, and until you have experienced a full bull-to-bear cycle you are not a competent investor.
- 80% of the lessons required to make money cannot be learned from a course — they must be learned through firsthand experience.
- Pay your tuition early and cheap: losses when you're younger and less capitalized are much smaller than when you have more money and more to lose.
- Speedrun your mistakes through experimentation and reflection — the goal is to have paid your tuition by making mistakes with small amounts, then take back multiple tuition fees as profits later.
- The typical tuition period is two to four years, or one full market cycle — most people who make money in their first bull run still pay the tuition at the bear market.
