## Key Takeaways

- The key filter for finding coins worth researching is outlier price action — coins that hold their ground or go up when the rest of the market dumps are showing a strong community and underlying bid that tends to continue
- Strategy 1 — Smaller exchange new listing tabs: check MEXC, ExBitX (ExBitX), KuCoin, and SafeTrade new listing sections regularly; these exchanges list emerging layer 1 and AI coins early before they graduate to Binance/Coinbase; Casper was found this way before a 50x move
- Strategy 2 — CoinGecko top gainers during market dumps: go to CoinGecko, filter to Gainers, top 1000 coins, 24h view; during a down day look for coins gaining or holding flat — compare their chart against the total market cap chart to spot outliers
- When evaluating charts from either strategy: look for coins making higher lows while the market makes lower lows, coins that have not gone down with the rest of the market, or coins breaking all-time highs while Bitcoin is at cycle lows
- After building a list of outlier candidates from price action, step 2 is fundamental research using the checklist covered in a later video — price action screens candidates, fundamentals confirm whether to buy
- Casper is the repeated case study: it was breaking all-time highs at the exact moment Bitcoin hit its 2022 cycle low, and Tyler (a course member) who found it via MEXC made a 50x return
- New coins with no chart history (under 1 month old) cannot be screened with this method yet — add them to a watchlist and return in 3-6 months to evaluate whether outlier price action has developed
