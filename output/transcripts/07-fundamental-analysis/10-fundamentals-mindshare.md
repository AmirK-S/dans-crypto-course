# Fundamentals - Mindshare

## Key Takeaways

- Mindshare (how many people are thinking and talking about a coin) is the fundamental driver of price — you make money by being in the conversion funnel that turns attention into buyers and buyers into holders who never sell.
- All publicity is good publicity in crypto — the TikTok algorithm and the crypto price algorithm work the same way: engagement (love OR hate) drives viral distribution, which brings more eyes into a conversion funnel.
- Solana's comeback from $10 to $100+ is the clearest mindshare case study: it was the third-most talked about coin in crypto even at $10, with second-highest developer count in crypto — this massive gap between mindshare and price was the entire trade.
- "Mindshare today is market share tomorrow" — if a coin captures top-3 conversational mindshare at a fraction of its deserved market cap, price will eventually close the gap as people who talk about it become buyers and holders.
- The marketing funnel formula: get attention → convert to buyers → convert buyers to holders who never sell — HEX used 4-hour long-form videos to convert haters into cult holders; Solana converted haters through a superior product experience.
- "FUD is a marketing funnel for good projects" — when bearish news comes out and a quality project's community debunks it, converts doubters into users who try the product, and demonstrates the gap between perception and reality, the eventual price catch-up is inevitable.
