## Key Takeaways

- The four key team attributes for crypto investing: (1) builds fast and pivots quickly, (2) motivation beyond money (can't stop working regardless of wealth), (3) genius founder archetype, (4) prior history of becoming top 1% at something.
- The "alien founder, Jedi team, cult mission" pattern: every great crypto project (Ethereum, Solana, Binance) has a borderline-obsessive founder who assembles a world-class team and treats the mission as a life's purpose — bet on these founders regardless of what their coin is currently building.
- A founder's motivation must be beyond money — if they can make $10M and retire to Miami, they will; only founders who see money as a scoreboard (not a finish line) will build through bear markets and keep expanding the project.
- Prior success in any domain is the best predictor of future success — find founders who became top 1% at something (sports, technology, academics) because the process of becoming excellent is the same across domains.
- "The less girls the founder looks like he can get, the better" — founders with strong social lives have less time obsessing over their product; the best tech founders are often introverted, autistic-energy types (Vitalik, CZ, Satoshi) who can't stop building.
- A great team in a mediocre niche can pivot to success; a great niche with a mediocre team usually fails — Binance started as just an exchange and pivoted to launchpad, academy, VC fund, blockchain, and NFT marketplace, making BNB one of the few multi-cycle outperformers.
