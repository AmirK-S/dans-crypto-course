# Volume

**Section:** Technical Analysis
**Loom ID:** bf878123563047f3a7a479d87017d951

## Key Takeaways

- Volume represents attention — in crypto, like any market, more eyeballs and more buying interest lead to higher prices; a coin with rising volume during a price uptrend is healthy
- The four volume signals: (1) price up + volume up = good (bullish trend); (2) price down + volume up = bad (panic sellers); (3) price up + volume down = bad (trend losing steam); (4) price down + volume down = good (holders not selling, trend likely to resume)
- A high-volume candle on a breakout is a key confirmation signal — it shows buyers are chasing price higher, which sustains the move; a low-volume breakout is suspect and often fails
- For meme coins and smaller altcoins, on-chain volume data is generally reliable and useful; for Bitcoin and major coins, beware of wash trading and derivatives volume skewing the data
- A 20x spike in 24-hour volume almost always represents buying interest, not selling — sellers don't organically create that kind of surge
- Volume works best as a confirmation tool in confluence with price action, not as a standalone signal
