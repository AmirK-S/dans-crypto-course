# Leverage Trading - Yes or No?

**Section:** Understanding Crypto Cycles + Price Action
**Loom ID:** 337f13be5ae14724856cdb9855b48dc5

## Key Takeaways

- Leverage trading has a 99.9% failure rate — for 99% of people the disadvantages (heightened emotions, liquidation risk, higher fees) far outweigh the two advantages (capital efficiency, reduced counterparty risk).
- The purpose of leverage is to trade with less money, not to get access to more money — using it to get more exposure is the wrong mentality and leads to losses.
- As a symptom of the market cycle, people shift from spot investing to leverage trading late in bull runs — this is precisely when leverage is most dangerous.
- The only time leverage makes sense is during the Big Long (early parabolic breakout), and identifying this requires top 1% analytical ability — if you can't identify it, avoid leverage entirely.
- Spot trading has a higher win rate because of: no liquidation risk, less emotional pressure, bigger wiggle room, lower fees, and naturally longer time frames.
- The longer the time frame of your positions, the higher the probability of making money — shorter time frames (trading) dramatically lower your statistical edge.
