# Sector Rotations

**Section:** Understanding Crypto Cycles + Price Action
**Loom ID:** a63e9d065dec4edc96b5bf917530d916

## Key Takeaways

- Crypto has become rotation-based: different sectors (Layer 1s, meme coins, DeFi, AI, GameFi) outperform in cycles typically lasting 30-90 days — being in the wrong sector at the wrong time destroys returns.
- Flavor-of-the-month coins that dominate Twitter (Rollbit, Unibot, Tau) consistently underperform going forward — the time to buy is when no one is talking about a sector, not when everyone is.
- There are two valid strategies: momentum trading (ride breakouts and pullbacks in trending sectors) or mean reversion investing (buy capitulation, sell near peak awareness) — mixing them is a guaranteed way to lose money.
- The best performing sectors across bull runs have been Layer 1s and meme coins — picking the fastest horse sectors gives the highest probability of outsized returns.
- Being early and wrong means selling winners to chase momentum (e.g., selling Solana at $20 to chase Rollbit) — hold winners even when other sectors look more exciting short-term.
- Mean reversion investing requires going where the crowd isn't looking yet — buy fear and capitulation, then wait for the sector narrative to become consensus before selling.
