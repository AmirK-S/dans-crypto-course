# Diversification is a Myth

**Section:** Understanding Crypto Cycles + Price Action
**Loom ID:** 5265de737c6843748b90f0763996e3df

## Key Takeaways

- Diversification does not work in crypto because all crypto assets are high-beta — they all go up and down together, unlike stocks and gold which move inversely.
- You become rich through concentration, stay rich through diversification — since you are likely coming to crypto to become wealthy, concentration is the correct approach.
- Having more than 7 coins in your liquid portfolio dilutes your mental bandwidth, making it impossible to get good entries, exits, or to track each coin properly.
- The maximum recommended portfolio structure is 2-3 long-term holds (Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana) plus 3-5 smaller asymmetric opportunities — not 25 coins you barely know.
- Holding 25 coins also makes it logistically harder to sell at the end of the bull run, which is the single most important action you'll take in crypto.
- Crypto is increasingly a rotation game: one sector outperforms for a period, then another — concentration into a sector lets you capture the rotation.
