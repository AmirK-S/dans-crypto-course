# Reflexivity, Funding Data

**Section:** Understanding Crypto Cycles + Price Action
**Loom ID:** fcabeb1e64aa4be3b9081a37fa33afc7

## Key Takeaways

- Bitcoin's short-term price action is driven by derivatives traders, while long-term price is driven by organic buyers of actual Bitcoin — these are two distinct systems operating on different timescales.
- Reflexivity is a two-way feedback loop unique to crypto: higher prices bring more buyers, which brings higher prices — this is why crypto is more volatile than other asset classes.
- Funding rate is the most important sentiment tool in crypto: positive funding means dumb money is bullish (sell signal), negative funding means dumb money is bearish (buy signal).
- Unlike all other analysis tools, funding data cannot suffer from alpha decay or manipulation — it measures the collective real positions of all traders on an exchange.
- At negative funding extremes (bottom of market), traders were paying 1% per day to be short — doing the opposite of these emotional retail traders is the correct strategy.
- Sentiment analysis using funding is an "almost guaranteed" way to identify bottoms and tops — humans always repeat the same emotional cycle (euphoria at tops, capitulation at bottoms).
