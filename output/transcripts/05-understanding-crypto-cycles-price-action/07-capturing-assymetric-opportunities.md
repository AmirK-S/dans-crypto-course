# Capturing Assymetric Opportunities

**Section:** Understanding Crypto Cycles + Price Action
**Loom ID:** c1baaa46f2344cb7bb9875b279dfbb17

## Key Takeaways

- Asymmetric opportunity means a little downside for a lot of upside — this is the only way to make significant money in crypto given all the additional risks (hacks, rugs, liquidations).
- Buy when no one is talking about the coin and the sentiment is at its worst — this is where downside is capped and upside is massive; the more people talk about it the less asymmetric it becomes.
- Most people buy a coin immediately after finding it and deciding it's good — the correct process is to find it, build a thesis, then wait for an asymmetric entry (fear, non-consensus, forgotten by Twitter).
- Holding 3-5 coins with full focus gives asymmetric entries — Toshi was bought at $2.5M market cap because it was the only coin being focused on at that time.
- In trading, achieve asymmetry through risk-to-reward: risk 1, make 3-4; at a 50% win rate this produces profit over time.
- The process: research → thesis → wait for fear and Twitter silence → buy → hold doing nothing → sell when thesis plays out or is invalidated.
