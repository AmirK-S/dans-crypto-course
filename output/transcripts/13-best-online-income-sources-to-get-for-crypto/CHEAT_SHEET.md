# Best Online Income Sources — Cheat Sheet

## Why Online Income Matters for Crypto

- Having consistent income makes **portfolio management psychologically easier**
- "If you solely rely on your crypto portfolio for money it's not very fun"
- With income, you can absorb daily losses ($10k, $20k in a day) without panic-selling
- Without income, every portfolio loss threatens survival → emotional decisions → losses multiply
- Core two-part path: earn online income → allocate into crypto during bull runs

## High Ticket Sales — The Primary Recommendation

**Definition:** Working as a remote setter or closer for online education/coaching businesses selling products priced $3,000 and above (typically $5,000+)

### The Two Entry Roles

| Role | What You Do | Income Range |
|------|------------|-------------|
| Appointment Setter | Reach out to warm leads, book sales calls | $3,000-$15,000/month |
| Closer | Take sales calls, convert prospects to buyers | $10,000-$50,000+/month |

- Commission structure: typically **10-20%** of deal size
- Commission above 20% = red flag, likely a scam
- Example: 10% of a $5,000 product = $500 per sale

## How to Land a High Ticket Sales Role

### The Primary Strategy: Networking (Not Cold Outreach)

- 70% of people were hired where they already had a connection (LinkedIn research)
- For desirable companies this rises to 95%
- Cold DMs, job boards, mass resume submissions = "in 2024, the worst way to land a role"

**Networking process:**
1. Identify people working in high ticket sales or at companies you want to work for
2. Request informational meetings — to learn, gather information, and build relationships
3. Ask "how can I help you?" — add value, do not only extract it
4. Generate referrals through these relationships
5. Do NOT directly ask for a job — this signals a lack of understanding of how the industry works

### Getting Hired: Key Principles

- Over-prepare for interviews vs the average applicant: "If you do what other struggling people do, you will struggle"
- Do not copy/paste outreach templates — use them as structural principles, not scripts
- Screen the offer carefully before accepting — does the company's goal align with yours? Can it actually pay you what you want?
- Do not jump at the first offer out of excitement

## The Core Sales Principle

**"What you say is garbage. What they say is gospel."**

Sales = asking the right questions to help prospects reach their own conclusions, not pressuring them.

- "A man convinced against his will is of the same opinion still"
- Your presentation should be no longer than **10% of total call length**
- Never assume anything about a prospect's situation — always ask clarifying questions
- Let the prospect do the talking; guide, don't push

## Red Flags to Avoid

- Programs charging $5,000-$10,000 and claiming to **guarantee job placement**: "Guaranteed placement does not guarantee jobs... a lot of beginners fall into that trap and lose a lot of money"
- Commission rates above 20%
- Companies that cannot realistically pay target income levels
- Offers requiring upfront payment for "training" before any work begins

## The Bigger Picture

High ticket sales serves one primary purpose in this course context: **funding a crypto portfolio**.

The end game:
1. Build consistent $10,000+/month online income via high ticket sales
2. Allocate that income into crypto during bear markets and early bull run
3. Let the portfolio compound through the bull run
4. Exit near the top with life-changing wealth
5. Tax-optimize before the gains are realized (see Taxes section)

"Having income makes managing your portfolio easier" — it is the foundation that makes everything else possible.
