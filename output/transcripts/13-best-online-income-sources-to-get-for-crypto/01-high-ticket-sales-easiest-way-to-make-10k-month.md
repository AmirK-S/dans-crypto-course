## Key Takeaways

- High ticket sales (appointment setter or closer for online coaching/education businesses selling $3k-$50k+ products) is presented as the fastest and easiest way to make $10k/month online — the instructor used this income to fund their first crypto bull run, which led to their first 7-figure outcome
- Two entry roles: appointment setter ($3k-$15k/month) who books calls from warm leads; and closer ($10k-$50k+/month) who converts prospects on live calls at 10-20% commission — anything above 20% commission is a red flag and likely a scam
- Networking is the dominant strategy for landing high-ticket roles — 70% of people are hired through existing connections, rising to 95% for desirable companies; cold DMs and job boards are described as the worst possible approach in 2024
- The core principle of sales: "What you say is garbage, what they say is gospel" — your job is to ask questions that lead the prospect to convince themselves, not to pitch them; your presentation should never exceed 10% of total call length
- Never assume anything about a prospect's situation — always ask clarifying questions to precisely identify their circumstances before making any recommendation
- Avoid paying programs that "guarantee job placement" in exchange for $5k-$10k — these do not actually guarantee employment and are commonly described as traps for beginners
- Having consistent online income makes crypto portfolio management dramatically easier psychologically — relying solely on crypto gains for income creates extreme emotional difficulty when positions go against you; having separate income allows you to hold through volatility
