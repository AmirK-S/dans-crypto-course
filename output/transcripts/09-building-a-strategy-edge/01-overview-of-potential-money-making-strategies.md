## Key Takeaways

- The recommended money-making strategies in order of priority are: long-term investing, airdrops, and presales — day trading and swing trading are explicitly not recommended for most people
- Long-term investing (buying low, holding 6-24 months, selling high) is the backbone of every serious crypto participant's portfolio and the strategy confirmed as most profitable by every crypto millionaire interviewed
- The strategy of holding a 5-100 million dollar market cap coin with strong fundamental conviction for two years will outperform margin traders, airdrop farmers, and pre-sale participants — confirmed by multiple 8-figure investors
- Capital-based strategy allocation: under $2k — focus on airdrop farming to build bankroll; $2k-$15k — airdrops + small cap coins; $15k-$50k — blend of long-term investments, airdrops, and presales; $200k+ — barbell (mostly safe majors, 10-30% high-upside small caps), automate airdrops only
- Free money opportunities (NFT whitelists, IEOs) appear near the end of bull runs when retail is abundant — keep an eye out but don't make them a core strategy
- Airdrop farming is very capital-efficient when done well — the Jito airdrop paid out $10,000+ to people who staked just 1 Solana (worth $20 at the time); manual airdrop farming is viable for those with time but no capital
- Staking and yield farming are not recommended because you are already taking on delta risk — capturing 5-20% yield means nothing if the underlying coin goes to zero, and the upside doesn't justify the opportunity cost
