## Key Takeaways

- The Hunter Algorithm was built after 3-4 years of studying all available data sets and concluding that funding rate data was the most unique and reliable — it does not suffer from alpha decay because the data reflects actual market sentiment regardless of how many people look at it
- The key insight: funding data alone is not sufficient because price can continue falling even while funding is negative; the combination of negative funding AND price being a certain percent below its moving average produces a reliable buy signal
- All four of the instructor's strategies (Hunter algo, presales, automated airdrop farming, 100x shitcoining) were built through networking — nothing was done alone; every strategy required a partner, coder, or networked community
- Presale edge is entirely a networking play: joining high-value communities, becoming friends with deal flow sources, and being able to fill deals at size to get first priority access — not something you can replicate from YouTube
- Automated airdrop farming was built by networking with a coder (Dino) who could automate a manual route designed by the instructor — 1,000 hours of research became automated across 900+ wallets
- Finding small coins (100x shitcoining) took 11,000 hours (6 years at 5+ hours/day) to produce the Fundamental 14 checklist — the time investment is the moat; the list only works because it took that long to develop the underlying pattern recognition
- When building your own strategy: use real data (not arbitrary moving average combinations), test over a wide parameter range to ensure robustness, forward test live before trusting it, and ensure the data actually impacts the market rather than just fitting past price action
